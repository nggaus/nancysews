Nancy's Teaching

Having taught at a local fabric store, I discovered that I really enjoy teaching, and my students seem to like my teaching style. I believe that everyone should at least know some basics and not be afraid to sew on a button or hem a pair of pants. I plan to teach mostly adults and youths that have completed sixth grade, since that is when most local kids get their first working knowledge of machine sewing in school. I want to keep groups small, especially for beginning students, so that I can give everyone the attention they deserve.

If you are interested in taking sewing lessons, please contact me for rates and availability. Most of my classes will be held at the Stacy Kate boutique in Baldwinsville.

Don't have a machine to use at home? I have several machines available for rent and plan to add that service to my business for prospective students.
